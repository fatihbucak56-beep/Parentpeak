# Parentpeak Investor Brief (EN)

- Status: Release-ready for the currently validated scope with staged rollout.
- Live validation: Security and Weekly Community smoke suites passed in production.
- Core value: Parents get fast, practical guidance plus a moderated community layer.
- Trust model: Verified expert voices and server-enforced moderation/review controls.
- Operational readiness: Monitoring, stop rules, and a clear go/no-go process are in place.
- Risk control: Hard thresholds for crash/API/payment health with explicit escalation paths.
- Launch approach: Staged rollout (10 -> 25 -> 50 -> 100%) with KPI gates between stages.
- Post-launch focus: Improve activation/retention and convert support learnings into rapid UX fixes.
